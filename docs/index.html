My name is Yixiang Yao (IPA /i:ʃiɔ:n jaʊ/), 姚一翔 in Chinese, a.k.a. YYX.

I'm an incoming Computer Science Ph.D. student at Center on Knowledge Graphs, University of Southern California, Information Sciences Institute, advised by Prof. Pedro Szekely. Before this, I worked for startup companies, enterprises and research institute for years.

My Research interests include but not limited to Information Extraction and Integration, Knowledge Representation and Reasoning, Information Retrieval, Distributed Computing, Cyber Security and Privacy.
